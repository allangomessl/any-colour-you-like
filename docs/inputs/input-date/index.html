Input Date



Show editable code

on date field, we have some shortcuts for best user experience e productivity, for example:

  • Increment / Decrement

    • 20 days: digit 20 and press UP once

    • 20 months: digit 20 and press UP twice

    • 20 years: digit 20 and press UP three times

      For decrement use DOWN key

  • Last / Next

    • Last 15: digit 15 and press LEFT once

    • Next 15: digit 15 and press RIGHT once

  • Partial

    • 1_/__/____: digit 1 and press TAB, then, it will complete to 01 of current month

    • 01/3_/____: digit 013 and press TAB, it will complete to 01 of march of current year
